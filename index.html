Pre-Operative Patient Education Video Suite

Developed a suite of pre-operative explainer videos covering: deciding on surgery, getting ready, getting organized for hospital, hospital stay, and recovery. This evaluation study aims to assess the impact of these videos on key outcomes for elective surgical patients within Sydney Local Health District, including anxiety, decision quality, care experience, and early postoperative recovery. Carers' anxiety and perspectives, as well as clinicians' experiences, are captured qualitatively to contextualize the quantitative results and inform implementation (Ongoing study).
