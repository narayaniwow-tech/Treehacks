Pre-Operative Patient Education Video Suite

A suite of pre-operative explainer videos "Deciding on Surgery, Getting Ready, Getting Organised for Hospital, Hospital Stay, and Recovery", has been developed to empower patients through every step of their surgical journey. This evaluation study investigates how these videos influence key behavioural and experiential outcomes among elective surgical patients within the Sydney Local Health District, including anxiety reduction, confidence in decision-making, overall care experience, and early postoperative recovery.
